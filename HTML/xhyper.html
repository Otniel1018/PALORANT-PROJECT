XHYPER

STORY

Nothing escapes his gaze. Nothing stays hidden for long.

Xhypher turns information into a weapon. With traps that punish carelessness and gadgets that keep constant watch, he controls the battlefield without firing a bullet. His strength is patience—while others scramble, he already knows where you'll run, and where you’ll fall.

You won’t see him.
But he’s already watching.

Cyber Cage

Deploys a remote-activated smoke trap that slows enemies inside.

Spycam

Places a hidden camera to scout and tag enemies from a distance.

Trapwire

Places an invisible tripwire that reveals and tethers enemies who cross it.

Neural Theft

Scans a dead enemy’s body to reveal all living enemies’ locations.

CYBER CAGE

Press [Q] to throw a cage that can be manually activated to create a smoke field that also slows enemies. Pair with trapwires or delay pushes.
