GEKKO

STORY

Gekko doesn’t fight alone—he rolls deep with his crew.
Each step he takes is backed by rhythm, by loyalty, by the little creatures that answer his call.

Hailing from Los Angeles, Gekko blends style and instinct, moving through combat with a casual confidence and his unique squad of creatures. While others rely on tech or brute strength, Gekko commands chaos with charm—every bounce, bite, and blast delivered with the energy of the streets he grew up on. His team isn’t just utility—they’re his family.

Gekko doesn’t just control the flow of the fight—he is the flow. When he moves, he moves with music. And when he strikes, it’s a whole vibe crashing down on you.

You’re not just facing one agent.
You’re facing the whole crew.

Wingman

Sends a buddy forward to concuss enemies or plant/defuse the Spike if it reaches a site.

Dizzy

Launches a creature that blinds all enemies in its line of sight after a brief charge-up.

Mosh Pit

Throws a sticky blob that explodes into a wide area of damage after a short delay.

Thrash

Equip a set of floating wind-forged daggers that deal precise, deadly damage.

WINGMAN

Press [Q] and FIRE to send Wingman forward—if it sees an enemy, it concusses them; if you’re holding the Spike and aiming at a site, it plants for you. You can also use ALT FIRE to defuse the Spike instead. Retrieve him after use to reuse the skill later.
