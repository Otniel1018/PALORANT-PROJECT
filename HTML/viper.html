VIPER

STORY

Viper doesn't just control space—she owns it.

With a mind as sharp as her chemicals, Viper poisons the battlefield with precision and patience. Her presence forces enemies to think twice before pushing, her toxins cutting off vision, movement, and even morale. Beneath her mask is a ruthless tactician who thrives in control, slowly choking her enemies of any chance to fight back.

You can run through the smoke.
But you won’t make it far.

Poison Cloud

Throws a gas emitter that creates a smoke cloud when activated using fuel.

Toxic Screen

Deploys a long wall of poison gas that can be raised or lowered at will.

Snake Bite

Launches a vial that creates a pool of acid, damaging anyone standing in it.

Vipers Pit

Emits a massive toxic cloud that stays active as long as Viper remains inside.

POISON CLOUD

Throws a gas emitter that emits a toxic smoke when activated. Can be picked up and reused, and consumes fuel while active. Use to block key sightlines or stall pushes.
