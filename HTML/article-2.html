PALORANT WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP 2025 ANNOUNCED

The first ever Palorant World Championship will take place in Toronto this June with a $2,000,000 prize pool. Qualifiers begin next month.

April 4, 2025

For the first time in history, Palorant is going global.This June, the city of Toronto will become the beating heart of competitive Palorant, hosting the inaugural Palorant World Championship 2025. With a jaw-dropping $2,000,000 prize pool, the stakes have never been higher. Legends will rise, empires will fall, and the world will witness the birth of the first true champions on the international stage.

Teams from every corner of the globe—hardened by fierce qualifiers and countless hours in the shadows—will finally emerge into the light. From the bustling streets of Bangkok to the steel cities of Berlin, from remote desert compounds to sky-high arenas, players have been preparing for this moment their entire lives. Now, the world is watching.

This isn’t just a tournament—it’s a war of style, speed, and strategy. Every duel will test nerves, every round will define reputations. For the players, it’s more than just victory. It’s about representing their origins, proving their worth, and becoming immortalized in Palorant history.

The qualifiers begin next month, setting off a global race toward greatness. Only the strongest, sharpest, and most synchronized teams will make it through the gauntlet. With agents clashing in iconic maps and meta-defining plays rewriting the rules of engagement, this championship is set to reshape the competitive scene forever.

Whether you're a newcomer looking to prove yourself, a veteran chasing one last shot at glory, or a fan ready to witness history unfold—this is the moment. The path is open, the arena is set, and the future of Palorant begins now.

So gear up, lock in, and light up the battlefield.
The world is watching. Are you ready?
