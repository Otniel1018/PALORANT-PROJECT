SAGE

STORY

In the heart of battle, where bullets fly and chaos reigns, there is a moment of stillness.
That moment... is Sage.

She walks through warzones with steady steps and a gaze that sees everything—every wound, every danger, every breath taken too soon. Enemies see her as the lifeline of her team. Allies know she is the soul of it. Where others panic, Sage stands firm—focused, unwavering, alive with purpose.

Her power doesn’t scream—it heals, it uplifts, it restores. With walls that reshape the battlefield and a touch that defies death itself, Sage turns the tide without ever drawing blood. Her presence brings balance, and with balance, comes control.

You might break her team.
But you’ll never break her.

Slow Orb

Deploys a field that slows and reveals enemy movement.

Healing Orb

Heals an ally or Sage herself over time.

Barrier Orb

Creates a solid, rotatable wall that blocks movement and vision.

Resurrection

Revives a dead teammate with full health at their location.

SLOW ORB

Press [Q] and FIRE to throw an orb that shatters on the ground, creating a slowing field. Enemies inside the field move slower and make louder footsteps. Useful for controlling choke points and punishing aggressive pushes.
