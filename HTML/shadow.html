SHADOW

STORY

You won’t hear Shadow coming—only feel him as the light leaves.

Shadow moves between worlds, hiding in plain sight and spreading fear with every step. A master of deception and unpredictability, he thrives in the unknown. While others fight head-on, Shadow becomes the shadows—striking, vanishing, and confusing the battlefield.

He doesn’t walk with his team.
He haunts them from above.

Paranoia

Sends out a shadow projectile that nearsights and deafens enemies it passes through.

Dark Cover

Casts an orb that becomes a long-lasting smoke, placed anywhere in range.

Shrouded Step

Teleports a short distance to a selected location within line of sight.

From the Shadows

Teleports to any location on the map after a brief channel.

PARANOIA

Cast a shadow in a straight line that nearsights and deafens any enemy it touches. Best for initiating or disrupting grouped enemies.
