NEA

STORY

In a world that moves too slow, she’s already gone.
That flash of blue lightning across the battlefield? That’s Nea.

Born in Manila and built for velocity, Nea doesn’t just run—she charges headfirst into chaos, faster than sound, faster than thought. While others calculate and plan, she moves. Her body pulses with raw electricity, her steps crackle with power, and her presence sparks panic before she even fires a shot.

She doesn’t wait for the fight to come to her—she is the fight. With energy that burns brighter the longer she runs, Nea crashes through lines, slides under fire, and leaves trails of light in her wake. Blink, and you’ve missed your chance.

She’s not just fast.
She’s untouchable.
And when Nea arrives… you’re already too late.

Relay Bolt

Nea hurls an energy bolt that bounces once, electrocuting and concussing enemies at each impact point.

High Gear

Nea channels her power to sprint at incredible speed and perform an electrified slide to reposition quickly.

Fast Lane

She throws two parallel energy walls that block vision and damage enemies who pass through them.

Overdrive

She unleashes her full electric power, firing a precise lightning beam while sprinting at maximum speed.

RELAY BOLT

Press [Q] and FIRE to throw an energy bolt that bounces once before discharging. On each impact, it releases a concussive burst that affects nearby enemies. Use it to disrupt enemy positions or safely check corners.
