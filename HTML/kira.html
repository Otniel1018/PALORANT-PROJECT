KIRA

STORY

High above the skyline of Seoul, the wind isn’t just weather—it’s a stage.
And Kira dances on it.

With unmatched agility and a smirk that cuts deeper than any blade, Kira doesn’t follow rules—she rewrites them mid-air. She doesn't overpower her enemies. She outpaces them. Every duel is a performance, and she’s always the headliner. For Kira, the fight isn't just about survival—it's about style.

When the storm rolls in, Kira doesn’t back down—she takes flight. She rides the wind, dashes through bullets, and strikes with blades that seem to appear from nowhere. One blink, and she’s gone. One breath, and you’re out.

You might hear her laugh before you even know she’s there.
And by the time you turn to look—she’s already behind you.

Updraft

Harness a burst of wind to propel Kira high into the air.

Tailwind

Instantly dash a short distance in the direction Kira is moving.

Cloudburst

Throw a small burst of swirling wind that expands into a cloud of mist, obscuring vision for a few seconds.

Blade Storm

Equip a set of floating wind-forged daggers that deal precise, deadly damage.

UPDRAFT

Press the [Q] button to jump high into the air. Can be used to climb to high positions or surprise enemies from above. Good combined with smoke so your jump is invisible.
